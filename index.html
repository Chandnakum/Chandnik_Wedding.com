WE ARE GATTING MARRIED SOON

BHAVIK CHANDNI

We first met on my birthday, March 8, 2023. That day, my heart faster when our eyes met. Then we spoke on the phone and soon after, we decided to get engaged on 19th March. On 27th April, during a night call, Bhavik said he loved me. We even sent love messages to each other on WhatsApp and Instagram.

Like all couples, we also had good and bad times. If we had any fights, the day felt bad, but we always sorted things out. We could talk for hours and hours, sometimes till late at night. After four months of saying we loved each other, we met face-to-face. I felt very excited. Our first hug felt so right.

Saying goodbye was very hard, but Bhavik said to stay strong. That day was very special. He calls me "Gandi" lovingly, and I call him "Meri Jaan". His calls make my day and help me sleep well at night. I don't have a father, but Bhavik gave me all the love I missed. I am so happy he is in my life, and I will always love him.
